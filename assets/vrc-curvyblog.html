Curvy fashion?

It generally refers to fashion for large or curvy sizes. It focuses on designing and offering clothing that fits and highlights the beauty of people with curves and larger sizes. This approach seeks to promote inclusion and diversity in the fashion industry.

Although there have always been XXL sizes, the perception of fashion in relation to these sizes has evolved, to the point of becoming very common standards in many brands.

Throughout history, plus size models have been key to the development of a real image of women in our society. There has always been controversy over the diversification of sizes, which led to a simplified classification of female bodies as "pear" or "apple" prototypes. However, today, plus sizes not only exist, but have also become a prominent presence in an industry that is finally recognizing the importance of plus sizes and their prevalence.

Although brands have announced in the past their intention to regulate sizing to cover all body types, unfortunately, this commitment has not been fully realized.

Curvy fashion has challenged preconceived notions of beauty by celebrating diversity of sizes and shapes. This revolution has inspired designers to create garments that enhance and embrace curves, promoting a positive and healthy body image.
According to a study carried out in the US, it is estimated that 37% of North American women wear sizes XXL.

Trends:

curvy fashion has introduced a variety of trends that have made women see their bodies in a healthier way and improved the relationship they have with their body and mind. Tailored suits, asymmetrical cuts and vibrant prints are just a few of the options that allow curvy women to express their style with confidence. In addition, the designers have incorporated strategic details, to be able to bring out all the natural beauty of curvy women in an elegant way.

Tips to Dazzle with Style:

  • Know your body: Understanding your body type is essential to finding clothes that highlight your strengths. Experiment with different styles and discover what makes you feel attractive and happy.
  • Proper fit: The key to looking good lies in the proper fit. Look for clothes that hug your curves in a flattering way, avoiding clothing that is too baggy or tight.
  • Play with accessories: Accessories can be your best allies. Belts, statement necklaces, and elegant bags can add a unique touch to your ensemble and accentuate your personal style.
  • Colors and Prints: Don't be afraid to experiment with vibrant colors and bold prints. These options can be the perfect strategy to make your outfit truly positive.

Inner confidence: Confidence is the best accessory. Show off with pride and wear each outfit safe in the knowledge that your style is unique and beautiful. Transmit confidence and believe in yourself. Remember that we are what we project with our personality.
